SI Ladder (C-SSRS)

  1. Passive: "Naisip niyo na po ba na sana hindi na lang kayo magising?"
  2. Active: "Naisip niyo na po ba na saktan ang sarili o tapusin ang buhay?"
  3. Method: "Naisip niyo na kung paano?"
  4. Intent: "May balak po ba kayong gawin?"
  5. Plan: "May plano na po ba — kailan, saan, paano?"
  6. Past attempts: "May previous attempt na po ba?"
⚠ Active SI detected. Ensure safety planning is done before patient leaves. Consider involving family/emergency contact.
